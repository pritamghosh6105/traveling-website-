Dashashwamedh Ghat

Dashashwamedh Ghat is a main ghat in Varanasi located on the Ganges River in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is located close to Vishwanath Temple

Shri Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is heavily fortified, so it is best to check the official Shri Kashi Vishwanath Temple Trust for access details and guidelines.

Manikarnika Ghat

Manikarnika Ghat is one of the most sacred ghats in Varanasi, India, located on the banks of the Ganges River. It is renowned as a major cremation ghat and holds immense religious significance for Hindus.

Sarnath

Sarnath is a revered Buddhist pilgrimage site located just 10 kilometers northeast of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.
