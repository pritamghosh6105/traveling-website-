Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is world-famous as the "Pink City

Amer Fort

A magnificent UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring stunning palaces, courtyards, and the mesmerizing Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace).

Hawa Mahal

Known as the Palace of Breeze, its unique facade of 953 windows was originally designed for royal women to observe street life.

Jantar Mantar:

A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring a remarkable collection of ancient, hyper-accurate astronomical instruments.

City Palace

A sprawling complex blending Rajasthani and Mughal architecture. Explore the private royal residences, art galleries, and the spectacular Pitam Niwas Chowk (Peacock Gate).
