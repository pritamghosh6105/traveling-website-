Coorg

Coorg is a breathtaking hill station in the Western Ghats. It is renowned for its mist-covered peaks, sprawling coffee plantations, dense tropical forests, and cascading waterfalls

Abbey Falls

A spectacular 70-foot waterfall cascading through dense spice and coffee plantations, accessed by a short hanging bridge.

Madikeri Fort

A historic stone fort originally built in the 17th century, featuring a museum and sweeping views of the town

Mandalpatti Peak

Located about 18 km from Madikeri, offering 1,600-meter-high panoramic views of misty hills (best explored via a 4x4 jeep safari).

Nagarhole National Park

A wildlife safari experience where you can spot elephants, deer, and tigers.
